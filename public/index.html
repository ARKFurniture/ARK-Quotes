How quotes arrive

1. Customer emails you at info@arkfurniture.ca with a photo and description.

2. Zapier detects the email and sends it to your backend automatically.

3. The AI analyzes the photo and message — piece type, finish preference, detail rating — and builds a branded quote.

4. You get an email notification with a link to review the quote here.

5. Click Approve and the quote is sent directly to the customer from info@arkfurniture.ca.
